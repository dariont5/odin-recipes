White Rice

Description:

This recipe describes the steps to make some white rice that is fluffy.

Ingredients

  • Uncooked white rice
  • Water

Steps

  1. Scoop out 2 and a half cups of white rice into the rice cooker pot.
  2. Pour water into pot and rinse the rice. Drain the water and any other debris.
  3. Repeat step 2 at least 3 times, until only rice remains.
  4. Add water up to the 3 tick in the rice cooker pot.
  5. Cook rice on normal
  6. Enjoy!
