Toasted Ham and Cheese

Description

This is the recipe for a toasted ham and cheese sandwich. It does not match the image.

Ingredients

  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Butter both sides of 3 slices of bread
  2. Make the following stack: bread-ham-cheese-bread-ham-cheese-bread.
  3. Cook in a pan on medium heat until one side is golden.
  4. Flip and cook till the other side is golden.
  5. Enjoy!
