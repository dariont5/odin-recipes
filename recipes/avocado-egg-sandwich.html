Avocado Egg Sandwich

Description

The following is a recipe for an avocado egg sandwich. The picture may or may not be accurate.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Avocado
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Cook 1 egg sunny side up on medium heat till ready.
  2. Cut an avocado in half.
  3. Make many avocado slices from this half and then spread them on one slice of bread.
  4. Add finished egg on top of avocado.
  5. Add salt and pepper.
  6. Add the last slice of bread on top and enjoy.
